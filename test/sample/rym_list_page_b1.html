From Here to Anywhere: Emo from the Corners of the World

A list by LumineTheViatrix

Categories: Obscure, Artist Names

[List381213] | +82 | Log in to suggest an addition

Emo, in all of its incarnations (except perhaps screamo, which seems to be a worldwide scene), has often been associated chiefly with the US, since most of the prominent emo bands hailed from there. However, there are several emo bands existing outside of the US, and this list aims to gather as many of them as possible (although right now it is very much incomplete).

Artists are first ordered by country, and then by alphabetical order. The genre(s) of emo the artist has created albums in are listed underneath each artist's name. (Do note that not all of the albums by the artists listed are necessarily emo)

Feel free to offer suggestions for this list.

The title of this list comes from the song of the same name from Last Day of April's Angel Youth.
