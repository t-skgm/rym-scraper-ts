Music for Ghost Hunting... Dark Coffee... National Forest Back Roads... Bogs... Late Nights... Twilight Zones...

A list by Road_To_Niflheim

Categories: Listener's Diary, Underrated, Obscure

[List822095] | +323 | Log in to suggest an addition

An estranged listening log. For releases that are dolefully autumnal: set on the edge of night and morning, the acre whence the pines open up to a field of sphagnum. Liminal, what lurks beyond the cellar door. Crawl spaces. Lost time.

This is more a personal exploration than anything. A celebration of the kinds of music that I most adore. This list will tiptoe through many genres--exploring releases both uncommon and familiar. Darkwave. Post-punk. Minimal Wave. Etc. For a more ambient selection check out my other list Uncanny Atmospheres.
